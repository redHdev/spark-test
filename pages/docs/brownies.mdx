### Section 1: Ingredients
- 1 cup butter
- 2 cups sugar
- 4 large eggs
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 1/2 cup cocoa powder
- 1/4 tsp baking powder
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 1 cup chopped walnuts (optional)

### Section 2: Equipment
- Large mixing bowl
- Hand mixer or whisk
- 9x13 baking dish
- Non-stick cooking spray
- Wire cooling rack

### Section 3: Preparation
1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
2. Grease the 9x13 baking dish with non-stick cooking spray.

### Section 4: Mixing the batter
1. In the large mixing bowl, melt the butter. Add sugar and stir until well combined.
2. Beat in the eggs one at a time, ensuring each egg is incorporated before adding the next.
3. Stir in the vanilla extract.
4. In another bowl, combine the flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt. Gradually add this dry mixture to the wet mixture, stirring after each addition.
5. If using, fold in the chopped walnuts into the batter.

### Section 5: Baking
1. Pour the brownie batter into the prepared baking dish, spreading it out to ensure it is evenly distributed.
2. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with a few moist crumbs.
3. Remove the brownies from the oven and place the baking dish on a wire rack to cool.

### Section 6: Serving
1. Once the brownies have cooled, cut them into squares.
2. Serve them plain, dusted with powdered sugar, or with a scoop of vanilla ice cream on top.

Enjoy your delicious homemade brownies!
